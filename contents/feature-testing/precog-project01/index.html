Precog has been Aquired by RichRelevance

After over two years in the Big Data Analytics space, Precog LTD has been aquired by Rich Relevance. Their team will go on to work on the next generation data analysis, of baking cookies and making icecream.

The Precog platform has been outsourced and can be found on github. And is in fact continue to be developed in the spare time of Precog engineers.

If you are one of the Precog Customers who needs support please, contact our support team for further assistance.
